How to Login to Your Fubo TV Account
===================================

Logging into Fubo TV is simple and can be done on any device. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

1. Open your web browser or the Fubo TV app.  
2. Click **Sign In** or **Login**.  
3. Enter the email address linked to your account.  
4. Enter your password carefully, ensuring Caps Lock is off.  
5. Click **Login** to access your dashboard.  

After logging in, you can stream live TV, check your subscription, and manage profiles.

Forgot Password? Here’s How to Recover It
=========================================

If you forget your password:

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page.  
2. Enter the email associated with your account.  
3. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.  

This process will restore access to your account safely and quickly.
